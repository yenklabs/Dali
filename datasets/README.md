| Dali Verification Taxonomy | [verification-taxonomy/](verification-taxonomy/) | [dali-verification-taxonomy](https://huggingface.co/datasets/yenklabs/dali-verification-taxonomy) |

Models support the evidence ecosystem. They do not replace it.
